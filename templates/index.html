Why Crowdsourcing?

Smarter Governance

Allowing institutions leverage the wisdom and expertise spread in the crowd.

Better Decision-making

Improving a project’s efficiency, legitimacy and diversity.

More Opportunities for Innovation

Opening problem solving to other kinds of expertise.

About Us

We start from the proposition that there is no one way of “doing crowdsourcing” but, rather, engagement must be tailored to each needs and circumstances. Through our Advisor and Knowledge-Base, we attempt to help decision-makers in the public and private sector make an informed decision about when and how to use crowdsourcing.
